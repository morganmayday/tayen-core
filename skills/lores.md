The Recall Lore skill has _options._ Your first rank in the skill grants you two options--if you're granted a specific option, ie Recall Arcane Lore, you can still freely choose the other.\
These options are non-exhaustive. You can take another Lore option, if you create and clear it with your Spinner.

#### Arcane
Arcane lore lets you recall things about magic and the practices related to it. You might recall arcane lore to identify a magical sigil, guess the effects of a curse, recognize a spell being cast, or recognize the handiwork of a specific notable mage or recognize the mage themself.

#### Espionage
Espoinage lore lets you recall things about the historical and present practices of espionage and intelligence. You might recall espionage lore to identify potential information leaks, assist in the process of rooting out a mole, understand how to enhance your cover, construct or break a code or cipher, or understand what information on your enemies would help you best achieve your goals.

#### Historical 
Historical lore lets you recall things about the past. You might recall historical lore to identify an artifact, recall a historical event, recognize a site of historical importance, or recognize a participant--living or dead--in a historical event. Generally, historical lore covers anything about a decade or further back.

#### Legal
Legal lore lets you recall things about law. You might recall legal lore to recall the letter of the law, recognize a crime or loophole in the definition of a crime, or understand the spirit of the law.

#### Medical
Medical lore lets you recall things about anatomy and medicine. You might recall medical lore to identify a specific illness, recognize a poisoning victim, administer first aid, understand the goals of a surgery, understand sanitary principles, or identify and treat a wound.

#### Military
Military lore lets you recall things about the historical and present practices of military logistics and tactics. You might recall military lore to identify weaknesses in your combat strategy, build or construct a strategy to break a supply chain, make use of grand strategy, or allocate forces well.

#### Occult
Occult lore lets you recall things about the dreaming world. You might recall occult lore to recognize one of the jasilon, lakonah, or konawen, call an intersomnial being, chart a route between dreams, or ward off a nightmare.

#### Political
Political lore lets you recall things about the political state of the world. You might recall political lore to identify a major modern figure and the influences they have, understand the sociopolitical impacts of a historical event or present action, or guess at someone's intentions based on their stated or known politics. Generally, political lore covers anything within your character's adult life.

#### Wilderness
Wilderness lore lets you recall things about plants, animals, and weather. You might recall wilderness lore to guess at near-future weather, identify a plant, animal, or natural poison, find shelter, or forage.
