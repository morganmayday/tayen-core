You're exceptionally specialised.

When you take this trait, choose any skill. If it isn't a primary skill for you, you may swap one of your primary skills out for it. You get a permanent +1 competence bonus to that skill.

II: When you salvage the chosen skill, you can expend an Action Die instead of an attribute point, but only once per scene.

III: The related attribute for the chosen skill gets a permanent +1 increase.

IV: You can salvage the chosen skill up to once if you've succeeded.
